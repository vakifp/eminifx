Cancellation Policy

Cancellation Policy

Independent Sales Representative may choose to stop trading with Eminifx, inc. at any time and request cancellation of their account by emailing support@eminifx.com or via the Message Center located in the backoffice.

Cancellation Request within 7 to 14 days

Cancellations made within14 calendar days of funding will receive a full refund, minus any trading or affiliate bonuses earned.

Refunds require 7 to 14 business days to process and will be returned in the same method in which the funds were paid. Values may fluctuate based on market value at the time payment is released.

Cancellation Request within 15 to 90 days

Cancellations made between 15 to 90 days of funding require a withdrawal request be made from the trading balances. Users may request a partial or full refund of their balances. The refund will include the current trading balance minus a 20% processing fee.

Independent Sales Representative is entitled to all previously earned trading and affiliate bonuses, unless otherwise indicated, and may withdraw those balances if still in their e-wallet at the time of the cancellation request. Eminifx, inc. reserves the right to review all balances for approval.

Refunds require 7 to 14 business days to process and will be returned in the same method in which the funds were paid. Values may fluctuate based on market value at the time payment is released.

Cancellation Request after 90 days

Cancellations made after 90 days of funding require a withdrawal request be made from the trading balances. Users may request a partial or full refund of their balances. The refund will include the current trading balance.

Independent Sales Representative is entitled to all previously earned trading and affiliate bonuses, unless otherwise indicated, and may withdraw those balances if still in their e-wallet at the time of the cancellation request. Eminifx, inc. reserves the right to review all balances for approval.

Refunds require 7 to 14 business days to process and will be returned in the same method in which the funds were paid. Values may fluctuate based on market value at the time payment is released.

Request to reopen a previously cancelled account

Independent Sales Representative may apply for re-application within 30 days, at which time the previously closed account will be evaluated for reopening. After 120 calendar days, the previously closed account will no longer qualify for reopening and the Independent Sales Representative will be required to submit a new application.

Independent Sales Representative further acknowledges that their account requires a trading balance of $99 AND be up to date on the monthly service fee - OR - have paid the $49.95 Marketing License AND be up to date on the monthly subscription fee for a period not to exceed 30 days in order to remain active.

Accounts that are inactive due to non-payment for a period exceeding 30 days will be closed and require approval for reopening. After 120 calendar days, the previously closed account will no longer qualify for reopening and the Independent Sales Representative will be required to submit a new application.
